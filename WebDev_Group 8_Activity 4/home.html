ADOBO

Manam’s Adobo is a slow-cooked delight of tender chicken in vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, and black pepper, enriched with caramelized onions, bay leaves, and a hint of coconut milk. Served with steamed rice, it’s a perfect blend of tangy, savory, and comforting—Filipino tradition in every bite.

KARE-KARE

Kare-Kare is a rich, velvety peanut stew with fork-tender oxtail, tripe, and crisp vegetables in a thick, nutty sauce. Served with bagoong for a bold contrast, this Filipino classic is creamy, hearty, and deeply umami— a comfort food that feels like home.

LUMPIANG SARIWA

Lumpiang Sariwa is a fresh Filipino spring roll filled with sautéed vegetables, shrimp, and savory pork, wrapped in a delicate crepe and drizzled with sweet, garlicky peanut sauce. Topped with crushed peanuts and crisp lettuce, it’s a perfect balance of flavor and texture—Filipino comfort food at its finest.
